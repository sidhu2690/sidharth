About

I am Sidharth SS, a dedicated research enthusiast pursuing my academic journey in high-energy astrophysics and gravitational wave physics. Fuelled by an insatiable curiosity about the mysteries of the universe, I am passionate about leveraging technology to unravel these enigmas. Currently pursuing an Integrated BS-MS in Physics with a Minor in Data Science at the prestigious Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Mohali, my academic pursuit is fortified by practical experiences and collaborations that have widened my scientific horizons. My involvement as an Associate Scientist within the SWGO Collaboration, working alongside the National Institute for Nuclear Physics - INFN Sezione di Padova and the University of Padova, has been pivotal. Together, we strive to optimize the Southern Wide-field Gamma-ray Observatory (SWGO) Experiment, contributing to the advancement of our understanding of the cosmos. Collaborating with esteemed scientists, including Dr. Tommaso Dorigo and Dr. Shashi Bhushan Pandit, has been awe-inspiring. These experiences have allowed me to witness the seamless fusion of technology and scientific exploration, further fueling my passion for scientific discovery. In addition to my academic pursuits, I actively engage in projects aimed at refining gravitational wave data analysis, exploring the phenomenon of peVatrons with the SWGO team, and contributing to the development of machine learning tools like CortexFlow. My expertise encompasses data analysis, machine learning, and proficiency in programming languages such as Python and R. I have developed tools like CurvPy, dedicated to enhancing curve fitting accuracy in data analysis. Participating in workshops and conferences, such as the Gravitational Wave Open Data Workshop and the 8th General Meeting of the SWGO Collaboration, has enriched my knowledge base, shaping me into an astute researcher. As a member of the Universal Scientific Education and Research Network (USERN) in Mohali, India, I am committed to contributing meaningfully to the scientific community. My goal is to continually push the boundaries of human understanding and make impactful contributions to the field of astrophysics. Join me on this journey as we explore the marvels of the universe together.
